⚠ Race Day Pacing

Strategy: Negative Split — Walk Only at Aid Stations

Sub-4h = 5:41/km avg. Run steady, walk only at aid stations (~30–45 sec each). No scheduled walk breaks.

Km 0–35:45–5:50DOWNHILL. You'll feel incredible. HOLD BACK. Don't bank time — bank energy.
Km 3–105:35–5:40Settle into race pace. First gel at km 5. Find a group running your pace.
Km 10–215:35–5:40Cruise. Gels on schedule. This should feel controlled, not hard.
Km 21–305:35–5:45Halfway. The real race begins now. Stay disciplined. Keep eating.
Km 30–355:45–6:00THE WALL. Glycogen depleting. Keep eating gels. 1 km at a time. Mantra.
Km 35–395:45–6:10Hold on. ~10 min buffer if on pace. Don't panic if you slow slightly.
Km 39–425:50–6:1550m uphill. Dig deep. The crowd will carry you. Empty the tank last 2km.
FINISH< 4:00:00Hotel Moskva. Sub-4. You are a marathoner.

Aid station strategy: Walk 30–45 sec at each station to drink properly. ~8 stations = ~5 min total walking. Running pace of 5:35 compensates.

Decision point: Week 4 long run (28–30km). If you hold 5:40–5:50/km for 25+ km with HR in Z2–Z3, sub-4h is on. If HR spikes to Z4+ or pace drops after 20km, we adjust to 4:15.

? The Unknown Unknowns

The Wall (Km 30–35)

Glycogen depletes. Brain screams STOP. Normal. Keep eating gels. 1km chunks. Have a mantra.

Bathroom Issues

Runner's stomach is common. No fiber 48h before. If you need to go — GO.

Chafing & Nipple Bleeding

Body Glide everywhere. Tape nipples. Test on long runs.

Pacing Discipline

#1 mistake: too fast in km 0–10. 6:00 instead of 6:45 early = death at km 32.

Toenails

May lose them. Trim SHORT. Cut Wednesday before race.

Nothing New on Race Day

Not shoes, socks, shorts, gels, or breakfast. Everything tested.

Mental Game (50%)

Have a "why." Dedicate km to people you love. "One more km" is all that matters at km 33.

Weather

April 19: typically 8–18°C. Dress for km 20, not km 0. Check forecast from Apr 16.

Post-Race

Shuffle-walk 2–3 days. 7–10 days off. Immune system suppressed. Rest.

Walking Is OK

5:30 with lots of walking = same medal, same 42.195km.

No Alcohol

Destroys recovery. Zero from Wednesday before race. Limit all 5 weeks.

Sleep > Everything

Night-before sleep will be bad — OK. Thursday night matters more.
